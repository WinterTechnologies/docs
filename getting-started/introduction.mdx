## About Winter HR

Winter HR is a free and open-source Roblox group management system It allows you to create groups, training sessions, forms, and more.

## Hosting

We currently do not provide a hosted version of Winter HR.
However, you can spin up an instance of it with relative
ease if you have knowledge of NextJS & Docker.
